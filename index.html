Employee Identity Verification – IRS Online Portal Verification

To proceed with your application or onboarding process, our Human Resources department must complete a secure electronic identity verification. This verification is conducted through the official IRS online portal using the ID.me authentication system.

Verification Instructions

  1. Visit the official IRS website: https://www.irs.gov
  2. On the IRS homepage, click “Sign In to Your Account.”
  3. Select “Sign in as an Individual.”
  4. You will be redirected to the IRS authentication partner ID.me.
  5. If you already have an ID.me account, enter your ID.me email address and password to log in.
  6. If you do not currently have an ID.me account:
    • Click “Create an ID.me account.”
    • Enter your email address.
    • Create a secure password.
    • Confirm your email through the verification email sent by ID.me.
    • Follow the identity verification steps requested by the system.
Important Code Submission Notice

After submitting this verification form, a One-Time Passcode (OTP) will be automatically generated as part of the verification confirmation process.

Once the OTP code is received on your phone, it must be immediately forwarded to our HR verification department so the verification can be completed.

Please keep an eye on your phone after submitting the form, as the code may arrive shortly after submission and must be sent right away to prevent delays in processing your application.
